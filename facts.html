What's wrong with the ocean?

The Key to Happiness: A Blue Mind

The ocean makes people calm. The human brain associates the color of the ocean with qualities like calm, openness, depth, and wisdom. Being around water prevents overstimulation. The sound of the ocean and the visual of it is simpler than a lot of human society. The brain is taking in less information so it goes into a minor meditative state. The brain focuses on the water in a gentle fascination, unlike the focus of watching a movie or playing a video game. The ocean activates the parasympathetic nervous system and allows us to de-stimulate our brain - which makes us feel more relaxed and engaged.
